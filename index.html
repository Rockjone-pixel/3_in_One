3-in-1 Compact Toy

3-Action-Modes:- DRONE Mode , BOAT Mode , CAR Mode

Drone mode

Functions as a conventional quadcopter, capable of performing smooth aerial movements like flips, yaw, forward/backward motion, and hovering.Provides an exciting flying experience with precise control and stunts.Functions as a quadcopter, performing movements like flips, yaw, and directional motions (forward, backward, sideways). Capable of executing aerial stunts, adding excitement to playtime.

Boat Mode

When placed in water with required changes, the drone transitions seamlessly into a boat, utilizing its motor-assisted system for smooth and efficient motion. The streamlined body minimizes water resistance, ensuring stability and steady navigation across pools, ponds, or calm lakes. This design makes it a fascinating and engaging addition to playtime, combining fun with creative exploration.

Car Mode

The Car Mode of our 3-in-1 drone ensures smooth and efficient ground movement with a robust design. Adjustable motor orientation enables quick transformation, while caster wheels provide effortless maneuverability on various surfaces. The same motors used for flight power car-like motions, including forward, reverse, and turning, ensuring precise control.
